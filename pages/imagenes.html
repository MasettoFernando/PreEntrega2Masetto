Festivales a los que te podemos llevar

Time Warp

After marking their 25th anniversary with a spectacular edition in 2019, the dancefloor is opening up once more so expect unrivalled lineups and exceptional production across the festival's two stages.

Verve Festival

Verve is a brand new and historic techno festival coming to Lucerne’s Allmend/Messe 1 arena in Switzerland. From May 20th – 22nd it promises something new and supersized for the city, with cutting edge artists in an innovative indoor space.

Awakenings

Set to take place on July 29-31 in Hilvarenbeek, Awakenings Summer Festival is sure to pack quite a punch with its massive lineup of techno greats. Immediate standouts on the bill include the likes of Stephan Bodzin, Enrico Sangiuliano, Chris Liebing, I Hate Models, Richie Hawtin, Charlotte de Witte, Adam Beyer, and Ben Klock, making it clear that each day is sure to be filled with stunning sets..

Terminal V: Resurgence

The huge indoor event boasts more stages than ever before, with six arenas featuring over 80 of the biggest and most in-demand international acts from all corners of electronic music.

Mint Festival

Mint Festival will be returning to Newsam Green Farm in Leeds this September to celebrate their 10th edition of the festival. You can expect the usual suspects on the lineup with an all-out energy in the crowd for this one.

MDLBEAST Soundstorm

MDLBEAST’s Soundstorm once again raised the bar for musical experiences in the Middle East with its latest festival. In all, 732,000 attendees from all over the world descended upon the desert with 184 artists from more than 20 countries playing over eight stages..
